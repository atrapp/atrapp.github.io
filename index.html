About me

Jr. Web Developer, IT-Professional, Social Entrepreneur

I am a recent graduate from the "Web Development Immersive" (WDI) course at General Assembly in New York City.

WDI is a 3-month Immersive Program focusing on training full stack web development. It teaches core technologies in front-end as well as back-end web development. For me, the course has been the perfect entryway into that field.

Originally I am from Hamburg, Germany, but I relocated to the United States at the beginning of 2014 and now I am permanently living in New York (with Green Card).

After graduating "FH Wedel - University of Applied Sciences" with a degree in Business Information Systems, I have been working in different industries and companies, with Specialist focus on Microsoft SharePoint and Lotus Notes Platforms. That was very rewarding, but now I am excited to begin my new career as a Junior Web Developer.

In addition to working in tech I like to engage myself in social areas. For example, I co-founded a business to share computer knowledge and experience to make technology accessible to others (especially older people) and volunteered in a Fair Trade Store to support Economic Empowerment in Third World countries. I am also the Founder and Owner of a Fair Trade boutique enterprise in the greeting cards and gift items industry in Germany, doing business since August 2012.

If you would like to know more, please email me or give me a call. I'll look forward to hearing from you. . . and maybe we can have a Fair Trade coffee together!

Thanks.
