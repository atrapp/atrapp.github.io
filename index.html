About me

The most important about me: I love Web Development!

And I am a recent graduate from the "Web Development Immersive (WDI)" course at General Assembly in New York City. WDI is an immersive training program over 3 months to transform beginners into Full Stack Web Developers by teaching them core technologies in Front-end as well as Back-end web development. For me the perfect jump into this field.

Originally I am from Hamburg, Germany, where I was born and lived until the end of 2013. In January 2014 I relocated to the United States and now I am permanently living in New York (I have a work permit for the USA).

In Germany I have been working as an IT professional for many years, after graduating the "FH Wedel - University of Applied Sciences" with a college diploma in Business Information Systems. I was employed in different industries and companies, and mainly working with Microsoft SharePoint and Lotus Notes Platforms. That was exciting, but now I am very excited to begin my new career as a Junior Web Developer. The Web is the future... and I want to be part of creating it!

Besides working in the technical field I generally like to engage myself in social areas. So, for example, I co-founded a business to share computer knowledge and experience to make technology accessible to others (especially older people) and volunteered in a Fair Trade Store to support Economic Empowerment in Third World countries. I am also the Founder and Owner of a Fair Trade boutique enterprise in the greeting cards and gift items industry in Germany, doing business since August 2012.

Now, I hope you are curious to learn more about me. If you like send me a message or give me a call. I am looking forward to hearing from you and maybe having a cup of Fair Trade coffee together! :)

Thanks.
